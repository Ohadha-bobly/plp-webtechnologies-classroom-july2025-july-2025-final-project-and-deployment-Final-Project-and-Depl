About AccessAble Solutions

Companies today often lack the awareness, expertise and processes to make their digital products and services accessible to the 15% of the global population who have disabilities.We are dedicated to creating a more inclusive digital world for everyone.

Our Mission

At AccessAble Solutions, our mission is to bridge the gap between digital innovation and accessibility. We believe that everyone, regardless of their abilities, should have equal access to digital information, services, and experiences. Through expert consulting, comprehensive audits, and inclusive design practices, we help organizations create digital products that truly serve all users.

Our Core Values

Inclusion First

We believe accessibility is not an afterthought but a fundamental principle that should be woven into every aspect of digital design and development from the very beginning.

User-Centered Approach

Real people with diverse abilities are at the heart of everything we do. We prioritize user research and testing with people who use assistive technologies.

Practical Excellence

We provide actionable, realistic solutions that balance accessibility best practices with business needs and technical constraints.

Continuous Learning

The accessibility landscape is constantly evolving. We stay current with the latest standards, technologies, and best practices to serve our clients better.

Our Story

AccessAble Solutions was founded in 2020 when Geoffrey Ohadha, after years of working as a UX designer, realized how many digital products were inadvertently excluding users with disabilities. What started as a passion project to help local businesses make their websites more accessible has grown into a comprehensive accessibility consulting firm serving clients across industries and around the world.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 200 organizations improve their digital accessibility, trained thousands of professionals in inclusive design practices, and contributed to making the web a more accessible place for everyone. Our work has directly impacted millions of users, ensuring they can access information, services, and opportunities online with dignity and independence.

We're not just consultants, we're advocates, educators, and partners in the journey toward digital inclusion. Every project we take on is an opportunity to create positive change and move closer to our vision of a truly accessible digital world.
