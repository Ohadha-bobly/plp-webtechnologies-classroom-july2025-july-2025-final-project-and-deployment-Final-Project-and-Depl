Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an accessibility audit take?

Most audits take 1-3 weeks depending on the size and complexity of your digital properties. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with small businesses?

We work with organizations of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies. We offer scalable solutions that fit different budgets and needs.

Can you help with legal compliance?

Yes, our team includes legal compliance experts who can help you understand ADA requirements, Section 508 standards, and international accessibility laws.

Do you offer remote training?

Yes, we offer both in-person and remote training sessions. Our virtual workshops are interactive and just as effective as in-person training.

What's included in your ongoing consulting?

Our consulting packages include regular accessibility reviews, development team support, policy development, and continuous monitoring of your accessibility progress.

How much do your services cost?

Costs vary based on scope and complexity. We offer free consultations to understand your needs and provide accurate pricing. Most audits start at $2,500.
